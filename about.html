Meet the Makers Behind J and A Masterpieces




Jonathan, a retired Army Veteran from Texas, spent 20 years as a Language Analyst, mastering precision, discipline, and attention to detail. After hanging up the uniform, he traded in his headset for hand tools—channeling his creativity into craftsmanship and design.



Audrey, a talented Cytotech from Georgia, brings a vibrant flair to every project. With a keen eye for color and a passion for adding depth and dimension, she transforms ordinary pieces into bold, eye-catching works of art.

Together, they're the heart and hands behind J and A Masterpieces. Based in the Augusta, GA area, they create artisan home goods, personalized décor, and creative media with the help of their two kids, two cats, and a shared love for bringing ideas to life.
